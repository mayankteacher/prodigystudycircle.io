Introduction

The National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 emphasizes the importance of high-quality teacher development. MMTTP aims to create energized, capable educators for higher education.

Background

The MMTTP scheme restructures existing mechanisms to establish 111 MMTTCs across India to implement NEP-aligned training programs for faculty.

Objectives

  • Empower educators through capacity building and leadership training
  • Promote Indian values, ethics, and sustainability in teaching
  • Align teaching practices with NEP 2020
